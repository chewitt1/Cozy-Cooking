Chai Latte

I love chai- all kinds of chai. Chamomile chai, Mint chai, lots of chai.

This recipe, however, is about Masala chai. Well, the easy, non-authentic, non-homemade version anyways.

It's very easy. You only need two ingredients. I used to add a third (sugar) until I realized I didn't need it at all.

Ingredients

  • Tazo Classic Latte Chai Black Tea
  • Milk (any kind and temperature- I prefer cold Oat milk)

Instructions

  1. Pour your milk in a cup (I like to do a 4:1 ratio of milk to black tea)
  2. Pour the chai
  3. At this point, you can add more stuff (sugar, ice, cinnamon sticks...) but this is where I stop
  4. Drink the yummy stuff, yay!
